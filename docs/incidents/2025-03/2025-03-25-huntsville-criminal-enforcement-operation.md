# 13 Arrested in Multi-Agency Criminal Enforcement Operation in Huntsville

## Summary
On March 25, 2025, ICE along with FBI, ATF, and DEA conducted an enhanced targeted enforcement operation in Huntsville focusing on individuals with criminal records. Eight of those arrested had been previously removed from the U.S. and had federal convictions for illegal reentry. Charges against those detained included domestic violence, narcotics trafficking, property crime, and DUI.
