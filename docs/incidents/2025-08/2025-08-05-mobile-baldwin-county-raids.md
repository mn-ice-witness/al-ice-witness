# 24 Arrested in Immigration Raids Across Mobile and Baldwin Counties

## Summary
On August 5-6, 2025, FBI Mobile confirmed immigration raids across Mobile and Baldwin Counties resulting in 24 arrests. Those arrested had prior convictions including forgery, assault, DUI, and immigration offenses. The operation represented continued enforcement pressure in the coastal Alabama region.

## Sources
1. FOX10 (Aug 6, 2025): [24 arrested in immigration raids in Mobile and Baldwin counties, FBI Mobile says](https://www.fox10tv.com/2025/08/06/24-arrested-immigration-raids-mobile-baldwin-counties-fbi-mobile-says/)

## Affected Individual(s)
- **Total arrested:** 24

## Criminal Backgrounds of Those Arrested
- Forgery
- Assault
- DUI
- Immigration offenses

## Agencies Involved
- FBI Mobile
- Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE)

## Context
This operation came approximately three months after federal officials announced they had reached 500+ immigration-related arrests in southern Alabama. Mobile and Baldwin Counties had become primary focus areas for enforcement due to construction industry concentration and proximity to the Gulf Coast.
