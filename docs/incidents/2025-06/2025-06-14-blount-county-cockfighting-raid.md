# 60 Arrested in Massive Multi-Agency Raid on Illegal Cockfighting Operation

## Summary
On June 14, 2025, the Gulf of America Homeland Security Task Force, USDA Office of Inspector General, and Alabama Law Enforcement Agency conducted a massive raid on an illegal cockfighting operation in Blount County. Six SWAT teams and two helicopters were deployed. The site housed over 250 birds bred for fighting. The investigation uncovered ties to gambling, drugs, tax evasion, firearms, and transnational criminal organizations.
