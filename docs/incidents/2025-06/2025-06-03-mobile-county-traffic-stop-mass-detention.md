# 28 Detained After Traffic Stop of Vans in Mobile County

## Summary
On June 3, 2025, Mobile County Sheriff's Office, alongside Homeland Security and FBI, conducted a traffic stop that resulted in the detention of 28 undocumented immigrants. The vans were pulled over for committing traffic violations. This represents one of the largest single-incident detentions in the Mobile area.

## Context
The detention occurred during a period of heightened enforcement cooperation between local law enforcement and federal immigration agencies in the Mobile area. The scale of the detention (28 individuals in a single traffic stop) suggests the vans may have been transporting workers to job sites.
