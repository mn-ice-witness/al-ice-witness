# ICE Raids Fultondale Home, Detains 3 Additional People Beyond Targets

## Summary
In late January 2025, ICE agents collaborated with Fultondale police to execute warrants at a residence during an early morning raid. Agents were looking for two specific individuals with outstanding reentry warrants. When they arrived at the house, they detained three additional people who were living in the home beyond their stated targets.

## Sources
1. WVTM13 (Jan 2025): [ICE raid confirmed in Fultondale](https://www.wvtm13.com/article/iceraids-alabama-immigration-raids-migrants-rights-birmingham/63611730)

## Affected Individual(s)
- **Primary targets:** 2 individuals with outstanding reentry warrants
- **Additional detained:** 3+ roommates/residents

## Agencies Involved
- Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE)
- Fultondale Police Department

## Community Impact
Allison Hamilton, director of the Alabama Coalition for Immigrant Justice, stated: "There's a lot of fear and panic in the community right now."

The raid sparked concern about ICE's practice of detaining individuals beyond their stated warrant targets when conducting residential enforcement operations.

## Editorial Assessment
**HIGH** - Confirmed by WVTM13 with statements from Alabama Coalition for Immigrant Justice. Part of early wave of increased enforcement following policy changes.
