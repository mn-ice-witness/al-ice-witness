# ICE Files Detainer on Honduran National Charged with Attempted Murder

## Summary
Javier Lopez Tinoco, a 20-year-old Honduran national, was charged with attempted murder for allegedly stabbing a victim three times during a public soccer game in Summerdale on July 19, 2025. He fled the scene but was arrested on July 29, 2025. At the time of arrest, he had 4 small bags of cocaine in his possession. ICE immediately filed an immigration detainer.

## Affected Individual(s)

## Criminal Charges
