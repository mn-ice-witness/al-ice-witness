# Multi-Agency Raids Target D.R. Horton Construction Sites Across Baldwin County

## Summary
From February 12-14, 2025, FBI, ATF, ICE, and Border Patrol conducted coordinated raids at multiple D.R. Horton housing developments across Baldwin County. More than 30 FBI agents participated in some raids. At the Jubilee Farms development in Daphne, a worker (Saul Cruz-Argueta) attempted to flee but was captured; he had been deported twice before. The raids marked a significant escalation of construction site enforcement in the region.

## Community Impact
Jail records showed immigration holds in Baldwin County quadrupled following this operation - from an average of 1 detainment per day under the previous administration to 4 per day. The construction industry reported significant workforce disruptions, with workers fleeing job sites in fear of raids.
