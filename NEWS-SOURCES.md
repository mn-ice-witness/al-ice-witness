## Key Communities

Alabama's immigrant population is concentrated in:
- **Birmingham:** Largest city, active advocacy organizations
- **Russellville/Franklin County:** 33%+ Hispanic population, poultry processing
- **Baldwin County (Gulf Shores):** Fast-growing, construction industry
- **Huntsville:** Growing tech hub, construction
- **Mobile:** Gulf Coast port city

## Recent Operations

- **Gulf of America Homeland Security Task Force:** Targeting construction sites
- **July 2025 restaurant raids:** 48 detained across 8 Mexican restaurants
- **September 2025 Russellville:** State-federal checkpoint operations
- **Leo Garcia Venegas case:** US citizen detained twice, federal lawsuit filed
